
To: Managing Director, Kochi Metro Rail Limited
Subject: Direction to Conduct Safety Mock Drill

Respected Sir,

In the interest of public safety and operational efficiency, the Ministry of Railways, Government of India hereby directs KMRL to conduct a comprehensive Mock Drill Exercise.

The mock drill should cover:

Track inspection and stability checks

Train condition and maintenance checks

Emergency evacuation drills for passengers

Fire safety and disaster response procedures

Communication and control system checks

Date of Drill: 5th October 2025
Reporting Deadline: 10th October 2025 (submit final report to Ministry)

Please ensure participation from relevant departments and maintain proper records of findings and corrective actions.

By Order,
Dr.Sharat Kumar
Joint Secretary, Ministry of Railways
